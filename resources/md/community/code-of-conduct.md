# uPortal Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The uPortal project is part of the [Apereo Foundation](https://www.apereo.org/) and follows the [Apereo Welcoming Policy](https://www.apereo.org/content/apereo-welcoming-policy). We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful.** Disagreement is not an excuse for poor manners. We all work on the same project and share common goals. Frustration is inevitable, but it cannot justify personal attacks. A community where people feel uncomfortable is not a productive one.
- **Be collaborative.** Our work is built on the contributions of many. Collaboration between individuals with different perspectives produces better results. When you disagree, try to understand why.
- **Be considerate.** Your work will be used by others, and you in turn will depend on the work of others. Any decision you make will affect users and colleagues, and you should take those consequences into account.
- **Communicate openly.** Invite new perspectives and welcome newcomers. Our community is strengthened by a diversity of participants, and every level of experience adds value.
- **When you are unsure, ask for help.** Nobody is expected to be perfect. Asking questions early avoids many problems and is a sign of engaged participation, not weakness.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Verbal, written, or physical abuse of any participant
- Sexist, racist, or otherwise exclusionary language or imagery
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Sustained disruption of discussions, presentations, or other events
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behavior

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it by contacting the [Apereo Foundation](https://www.apereo.org/content/apereo-welcoming-policy) or the uPortal Steering Committee. All reports will be handled with discretion.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all uPortal community spaces, including mailing lists, issue trackers, code review, chat channels, events, and any other forums the community uses for communication. It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce this code of conduct may be temporarily or permanently removed from the project team. Participants asked to stop any unacceptable behavior are expected to comply immediately.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is informed by the [Apereo Welcoming Policy](https://www.apereo.org/content/apereo-welcoming-policy) and the [Apache Software Foundation Code of Conduct](https://www.apache.org/foundation/policies/conduct.html), both of which guide open-source communities in fostering respectful and productive collaboration.
